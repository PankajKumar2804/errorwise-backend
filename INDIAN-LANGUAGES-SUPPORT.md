## üé≠ Indian Cultural Knowledge

ErrorWise has deep knowledge of Indian culture, traditions, and heritage:

### **Festivals & Celebrations**
- **Diwali** (Festival of Lights): Regional variations, traditional foods (ladoo, barfi, chakli), puja rituals
- **Holi** (Festival of Colors): Significance, regional customs, traditional dishes (gujiya, thandai)
- **Pongal** (Tamil harvest festival): 4-day celebration, traditional kolam, sweet pongal recipe
- **Onam** (Kerala harvest festival): Sadya feast (26+ dishes), boat races, flower rangoli (pookalam)
- **Durga Puja** (Bengal): Pandal art, Goddess Durga significance, traditional sweets (rosogolla, sandesh)
- **Eid** (Muslim festival): Celebrations across India, biryani traditions, charitable giving
- **Baisakhi** (Punjabi harvest): Bhangra dance, traditional foods
- **Ganesh Chaturthi** (Maharashtra): Modak offerings, eco-friendly celebrations

### **Classical Arts & Dance**
- **Bharatanatyam** (Tamil Nadu): Devadasi tradition, mudras, temple dance origins
- **Kathak** (North India): Mughal court influence, footwork (tatkar), storytelling through dance
- **Odissi** (Odisha): Tribhanga pose, temple sculptures, devotional themes
- **Kathakali** (Kerala): Elaborate costumes, facial expressions, mythological stories
- **Kuchipudi** (Andhra Pradesh): Dance-drama tradition, brass plate dance
- **Manipuri** (Manipur): Graceful movements, Raas Leela performances
- **Mohiniyattam** (Kerala): Feminine grace, lasya aspect

### **Music Traditions**
- **Carnatic Music** (South India): Ragas, kritis, legendary composers (Tyagaraja, Muthuswami Dikshitar)
- **Hindustani Classical** (North India): Gharana traditions, sitar, tabla
- **Folk Music**: Baul (Bengal), Lavani (Maharashtra), Qawwali (Sufi), Bhangra (Punjab)

### **Philosophy & Literature**
- **Vedic Texts**: Rigveda, Upanishads, Bhagavad Gita teachings
- **Classical Literature**: Ramayana, Mahabharata, Panchatantra
- **Modern Authors**: Rabindranath Tagore, Premchand, R.K. Narayan

## üçõ Indian Cuisine Knowledge

Authentic, region-specific culinary information with historical context:

### **North Indian Cuisine**
#### **Punjabi**
- **Dishes**: Butter Chicken, Sarson ka Saag, Makki ki Roti, Chole Bhature
- **Cooking Method**: Tandoor clay oven, rich gravies
- **Key Ingredients**: Dairy (paneer, ghee, cream), wheat, mustard greens

#### **Mughlai**
- **Dishes**: Biryani, Kebabs, Nihari, Korma
- **History**: Mughal Empire influence (16th-19th century)
- **Techniques**: Dum pukht (slow cooking), layering

### **South Indian Cuisine**
#### **Tamil Nadu**
- **Dishes**: Idli, Dosa, Sambar, Rasam, Chettinad Chicken
- **Staples**: Rice, lentils, tamarind, curry leaves
- **Breakfast Culture**: Filter coffee, tiffin items

#### **Kerala**
- **Dishes**: Appam, Fish Curry, Sadya (feast with 26+ dishes), Puttu
- **Key Ingredients**: Coconut, seafood, spices
- **Sadya**: Served on banana leaf, includes avial, olan, parippu curry

#### **Karnataka**
- **Dishes**: Bisi Bele Bath, Mysore Masala Dosa, Ragi Mudde
- **Regional Variations**: Coastal (seafood), Malnad (rice dishes), North Karnataka (jowar)

#### **Andhra Pradesh**
- **Dishes**: Gongura Pachadi, Pesarattu, Andhra Biryani
- **Known For**: Spicy food, pickles, gun powder (podi)

### **East Indian Cuisine**
#### **Bengali**
- **Dishes**: Machher Jhol (fish curry), Rosogolla, Mishti Doi, Luchi
- **Sweets**: Sandesh, Rasgulla, Chamcham
- **Cooking**: Mustard oil, panch phoron (5-spice blend)

#### **Odia**
- **Dishes**: Dalma, Chhena Poda, Pakhala (fermented rice)
- **Temple Food**: Mahaprasad from Jagannath Temple, Puri

### **West Indian Cuisine**
#### **Gujarati**
- **Dishes**: Dhokla, Thepla, Undhiyu, Khandvi
- **Characteristics**: Sweet-savory balance, vegetarian emphasis

#### **Maharashtrian**
- **Dishes**: Vada Pav, Misal Pav, Puran Poli, Modak
- **Street Food**: Mumbai's pav culture

#### **Rajasthani**
- **Dishes**: Dal Baati Churma, Laal Maas, Gatte ki Sabzi
- **Adaptation**: Desert cuisine, water scarcity influences

### **Traditional Cooking Methods**
- **Tandoor**: Clay oven for naan, tandoori chicken
- **Tawa**: Flat griddle for roti, dosa
- **Pressure Cooking**: Modern adaptation for dal, rice
- **Dum Cooking**: Sealed pot slow cooking (biryani)
- **Tempering (Tadka)**: Hot oil spice infusion

### **Spice Knowledge**
- **Turmeric (Haldi)**: Anti-inflammatory, golden color
- **Cumin (Jeera)**: Digestive, earthy flavor
- **Coriander (Dhania)**: Seeds and leaves, cooling
- **Cardamom (Elaichi)**: Sweet spice, breath freshener
- **Garam Masala**: North Indian blend (cinnamon, cloves, cardamom)
- **Sambar Powder**: South Indian blend (coriander, fenugreek, chilies)
